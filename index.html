Challenges in the Mining Industry

Identifying and solving the most pressing issues faced by mining operations.

Equipment Downtime

One of the costliest issues in mining is the downtime of critical equipment such as crushers, shovels, and loaders, often caused by G.E.T. components.

Inconsistent Blasting

Improper placement or incorrect amounts of dynamite can lead to inconsistent blasting, resulting in large boulders, medium fragments, and very small fragments, each causing different operational challenges.
